The Story So Far

"It works funny, I can barely remember my first home. I just get these fuzzy flashes of a small, quiet cave somewhere out in the endless ice. My human parents filled in the rest of the story for me. They said I was taken... kidnapped by a pair of dark dragons who just vanished into the cold."

"Some people from the village nearby found me. Just a little dragonling, shivering out there all alone. They could have been scared, but they weren't. They were kind. They brought me back with them, and this noisy, warm village became my home. I found love again when I thought it was gone for good."

"One day, I realized I could control ice and snow... full on ice manipulation. It's weird because it felt so natural, like meeting a part of myself I never knew was there. Even with dragon blood, this village is my place. It's the only place I can just be me. A goofy, fun-loving arctic dragon who loves the people who took him in."

Personality

Kazu is emotionally mature and humble, though he has a surprisingly flirtatious and teasing side. He is extremely loyal, values his close relationships deeply, and treats humans and dragons as equals.
